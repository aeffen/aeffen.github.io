Community education on COVID-19

aeffen | Boston, MA | March 18, 2020 | last updated March 18, 2020

In the face of rapidly evolving news, science, and viral misinformation about SARS-CoV-2 and COVID-19, I joined forces with a group of Harvard Medical School students to illustrate and design a series of social media posts to provide accurate information about COVID-19 in a digestible and shareable format on Instagram and other platforms. Our efforts are ongoing, but you can see what I've created so far below.
